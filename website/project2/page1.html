Pride of the Southland Marching Band

Tennessee's Marching Band, The Pride of the Southland, has been performing at halftime shows for over 110 years. They are regarded as one of the top marching bands in the nation. The Pride of the Southland has played at every Presidential Inguration (except 2013) since 1965.

Rocky Top

One of my favorite Volunteer traditions is singing Rocky Top. Rocky Top has been played by the school band since the early 1970s. While Rocky Top is not the offical Tennessee fight song (Down the Field remains the offical fight song), it is the most popular and iconic song associated with the Tennessee Volunteers.
